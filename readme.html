Theme Documentation for Holding Pattern

Holding Pattern is a very simple and easy-to-use landing page theme that allows you to quickly customize your landing page with your company logo, a custom background image and color, and allow people to subscribe to your feedburner feed either by RSS or email, if desired.

Customizing Holding Pattern

Customizing this theme is extremely easy.

Simply go over to the Colors & Images page to get started. From there you will be able to upload images to the theme to customize its appearance.

Once you've uploaded images and set background colors, go ahead and set up your Layout & Display options, as well as your General options as well, which contains the message that will be displayed to your visitors.
